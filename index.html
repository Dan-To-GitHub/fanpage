Bio

Blackpink are a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment in 2016. They are the first music act to have five music videos each gain 1 billion views on YouTube, and they have collaborated with numerous artists such as Dua Lipa, Selena Gomez, and Lady Gaga.
